About The Department

The Department of Computer Science provides high-quality education and research opportunities in software engineering, artificial intelligence, data science, and networking. We prepare students for modern technological challenges.



Read more about our mission

Our mission is to foster innovation,critical thinking, and problem solving skills through cutting-edge research and industry collaboration.
